Cho-Han Instructions

Cho-Han is a traditional Japanese dice gambling game.

It is a game which envolves betting on whether the outcome of a dice roll will be "Cho" (even) or "Han" (odd).

To play simply:

  • Select which outcome you'd like to wager on
  • Enter an amount to bet that is greater than 0 but does not exceed your current point amount

You can play until you run out of points!
